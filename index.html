PCB Technology

What are PCBs?

Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are the foundation of all modern electronic devices. They provide mechanical support and electrical connections for electronic components using conductive tracks, pads, and other features etched from copper sheets laminated onto a non-conductive substrate.

Our PCB Technology

At FRÈSH-TÈÇ, we utilize high-quality, multi-layered PCBs with advanced surface mount technology (SMT) to create compact, efficient, and reliable electronic systems. Our PCBs feature:

  • High-density interconnect (HDI) technology
  • Flexible and rigid-flex designs for versatile applications
  • Lead-free HASL and ENIG surface finishes
  • Thermal management solutions for high-power applications

Manufacturing Process

Our PCB manufacturing process involves several precise steps:

  1. Design and layout using advanced CAD software
  2. Printing the circuit pattern
  3. Etching to remove unwanted copper
  4. Drilling holes for component connections
  5. Applying solder mask and silkscreen
  6. Testing for continuity and functionality
  7. Component assembly and soldering
  8. Final quality control and testing
